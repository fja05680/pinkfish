Global Equities Momentum (GEM)¶

Gary Antonaccis Dual Momentum approach is simple: by combining both relative momentum and absolute momentum (i.e. trend following), Dual Momentum seeks to rotate into areas of relative strength while preserving the flexibility to shift entirely to safety assets (e.g. short-term U.S. Treasury bills) during periods of pervasive, negative trends.

https://blog.thinknewfound.com/2019/01/fragility-case-study-dual-momentum-gem/
https://seekingalpha.com/article/4010394-prospecting-dual-momentum-gem
